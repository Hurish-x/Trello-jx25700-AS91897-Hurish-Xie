Here are some tips for you before go to NZ

01 Weather

Check the weather before you travel, because New Zealand’s conditions can change quickly, even in one day.

02 Clothes

Pack layers and a waterproof jacket so you are ready for both sunny weather and sudden rain.

03 Shoes

Wear comfortable walking shoes, because many of New Zealand’s best places to visit involve walking outdoors.

04 Booking

Book popular accommodation and activities early, especially if you are travelling during the holiday season.

05 Driving

Drive carefully and always keep to the left side of the road, especially on narrow or winding routes.

06 Time

Leave extra time for travel, because some roads are slower and longer than they may appear on a map.

07 Respect

Respect nature by following local rules, staying on marked paths, and keeping outdoor areas clean.

08 Culture

Learn a little about Māori culture before your trip, as it is an important part of New Zealand’s identity.

09 Emergency

Call One-One-one (111) when you need an emergency response from Police, Fire or Ambulance if necessery.
