Here are some tips for you before go to NZ

  1. Check the weather before you travel, because New Zealand’s conditions can change quickly, even in one day
  2. Pack layers and a waterproof jacket so you are ready for both sunny weather and sudden rain.
  3. Book popular accommodation and activities early, especially if you are travelling during the holiday season.
  4. Bring sunscreen, sunglasses, and a hat, as the sun in New Zealand can be much stronger than expected.
  5. Drive carefully and always keep to the left side of the road, especially on narrow or winding routes
  6. Leave extra time for travel, because some roads are slower and longer than they may appear on a map.
  7. Respect nature by following local rules, staying on marked paths, and keeping outdoor areas clean.
  8. Learn a little about Māori culture before your trip, as it is an important part of New Zealand’s identity
  9. Keep your phone charged and download maps or travel information in case you lose signal in remote places.
  10. Wear comfortable walking shoes, because many of New Zealand’s best places to visit involve walking outdoors.
