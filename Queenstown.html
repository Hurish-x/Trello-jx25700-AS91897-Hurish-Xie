Dive Deeper into each destination

Skyline

Skyline Queenstown offers a scenic gondola ride to the top of Bob’s Peak, with panoramic views over Lake Wakatipu and the surrounding mountains. It also features the famous luge ride for a fun downhill experience.

Milford Sound

Milford Sound is one of New Zealand’s most iconic natural attractions, known for its dramatic cliffs, waterfalls, and wildlife. It is often described as one of the most beautiful places in the world. Read more

Shotover Jet

Shotover Jet is a thrilling jet boat ride through narrow canyon walls at high speed. It is one of Queenstown’s most famous adventure experiences.

Arrowtown

Arrowtown is a historic gold mining town with charming streets, boutique shops, and beautiful autumn scenery. It offers a peaceful contrast to busy Queenstown.

Lake Wakatipu

Lake Wakatipu is a stunning lake surrounded by mountains, forming the heart of Queenstown. It is perfect for relaxing walks, photography, and scenic views.
