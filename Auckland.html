Sky Tower

Sky Tower is one of Auckland’s most famous landmarks. It offers amazing city views and is a great place to start exploring Auckland.

Waiheke Island

Waiheke Island is known for its beautiful beaches, vineyards and relaxing atmosphere. It is a perfect destination for a peaceful day trip.

Rangitoto Island

Rangitoto Island is a volcanic island with unique walking tracks and stunning views. It shows the natural side of Auckland.

Devonport

Devonport is a charming seaside area with small shops, cafes and lovely coastal views. It is a great place to enjoy a quieter side of the city.

Piha Beach

Piha Beach is famous for its black sand, strong waves and dramatic scenery. It is one of the most iconic natural attractions near Auckland.
